# Assist Platform Enterprise roadmap

This roadmap sequences the outcomes in `PRODUCT_BACKLOG.md`. It does not create
a second backlog. Status and acceptance evidence remain authoritative there.

## Now — consolidate and stabilise

- Complete unified administration, global/brand context and permission-scoped
  brand switching without replacing the current UX redesign.
- Finish semantic design-token migration and rendered mobile/desktop acceptance.
- Complete Data Sources and Data Intelligence review-first workflows.
- Close production defects, monitoring gaps and backup/restore rehearsal.
- Keep LocalTorque private but production-capable pending domain and launch gates.

## Next — provider launch and marketplace value

- Complete canonical provider, claim, verification and multi-brand relevance flows.
- Implement Launch Access, Free, Founding Verified, Verified and Featured
  entitlements with billing disabled.
- Finalise provider launch templates, segmentation, previews and safe test sends.
- Improve national coverage, provenance, duplicate handling and zero-result actions.
- Complete brand-critical journeys for VanAssist, TowSmart and TrailerWise.

## Then — controlled commercial activation

- Configure and verify transactional email, sender DNS, bounces and unsubscribe.
- Obtain legal/tax acceptance and configure the selected billing gateway.
- Rehearse membership transition notices and failed-payment/grace behaviour.
- Activate billing only after the Commercial and full Platform Quality Gates pass.
- Measure claim, verification, membership and retention funnels.

## Later — enterprise and sale readiness

- Mature Brand Builder from reviewed private blueprints into controlled promotion.
- Complete the Social Studio template catalogue and editorial workflow.
- Produce the operational/data-room index, licence inventory and transfer runbook.
- Add external search, AI or distributed services only when measured needs and an
  accepted ADR justify them.

## Release rule

Roadmap position never overrides safety. Production work follows
`OPERATIONS_RUNBOOK.md`, requires evidence under `PLATFORM_QUALITY_GATE.md` and
must preserve a tested rollback path.
